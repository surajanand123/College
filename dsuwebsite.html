DSU DIFFERENTIATOR

Curriculum

Curriculum

Our curriculum is developed with our philosophy to quality education. Its salient features are that there is coherence in learning, it synthesizes experience and integrates education with experience. The curriculum is designed such that students get constant exposure to industry ready skills and techniques in conjunction with the theoretical fare that is on par with the best in the world.
Board of Studies

Board of Studies

Every department is guided by a Board of Studies (BOS), with an eclectic mix of theoreticians and practitioners from the academia and industry. Each BOS is blessed with the brightest of minds from premier institutes like Indian Institute of Science (IISc); Indian Institute of Technology(IIT) and central research institutes. Almost all of our Board of Studies panel is headed by Professors from the Indian Institute of Science.
Teaching Learning Process

Teaching Learning Process

We embrace the active learning paradigm for the delivery of our lectures. High quality lecture notes as part of the course pack based on the active learning paradigm have been developed exclusively by our faculty. Courses are delivered using our customization of MOODLE, DOODLE. PIAZZA is integrated into DOODLE to facilitate connection with the faculty beyond the classroom. Learning never stops at DSU.
Industry Driven Academics on Campus

Industry Driven Academics on Campus

Industry leaders like Autodesk, Bosch Etas, Bosch Rexroth, IBM, VMware, Nvidia and Intel have set up training academies on campus to train students from all over India in the latest technologies. Students at DSU benefit by being taking these certification courses, with possibilities for working on live research projects using them.
Research

Research

With a plethora of doctorates from IITs, IISc. and TIFR, research at DSU compares with the best that India has to offer. Innovative research projects on Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Machine Learning, Signal Processing, Nano Materials and Devices, Thin Films, Composites design all offer a rich stimulating research environment.
Faculty

Faculty

Our faculty are chosen after a rigorous selection process and many of them are from prestigious institutions in India and abroad. It is not uncommon to find PhDs from IITs and IISc teaching basic courses. What differentiates our faculty is the genuine empathy they have for their students. In the success of their students lies their satisfaction.
Entrepreneurship

Entrepreneurship

A young individual now has the option to enter the portals of DSU to be trained as a professional Engineer in a selected domain or opt to become an Engineer-Entrepreneur. A good part of the 4 lakh sq. ft space in DSU's Campus 3, has the provision to accommodate about 200 enterprises offering all the needed support and facilities for an idea to be translated into a product. This is one of its kind initiative, exclusive at DSU.
